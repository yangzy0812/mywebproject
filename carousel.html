Project

We live in a world of disjointed, uneven terrain. Many species of the world cannot move fairly, securely, and freely. Many species do not have equal access to opportunities, resources, transportation, and are therefore isolated and disconnected. It is this uneven terrain that drives us and informed the approach for this project.
