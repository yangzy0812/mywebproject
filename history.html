California

has a mild climateand abundant natural resources, where native Americans settled here. Then Spanish colonized the land, killed indigenous people and exploit natural resources. American and european also came here for trade and profits. Gold rush brings more people overseas to california, especially Asian immigrant. They came here to pursue the opportunity. But many of those immigrants moved by oppression. They move because of wars from the mainland countries(Muslim people), because of lack of life resources(east Asian), because of oppression from the government(middle eastern), etc. Nowadays, people are still migrant from countries all over the world and settle in California, which creates a high social diversity in the land. While as more people moved in, the Valley became home to many identities, inequalities, contentions, and disparities.
